for you

I look at you in this picture,
With those perfect waves of hair,
A cute little nose, a gentle smile,
And a gaze that stops me right there.

Your lips look ready to laugh or speak,
With a voice that is soft and low,
But it’s what goes on inside your mind
That I really want to know.

You have this wild imagination,
And a deep way of thinking things through,
Every single word you choose to say
Makes me want to get closer to you.

But I know I have to be careful,
Because you’re studying psychology, too,
Are you reading my mind this very second?
Is that what you’re trying to do?

It’s like a psycho studying a psycho,
A beautifully dangerous game,
You’re analyzing my every move,
While I’m just spellbound by your name.

You might be tracking my behavioral traits,
To see what makes me start to fall,
But you don’t need a textbook to figure out
That you’re the prettiest girl of them all.

So go ahead and study my heart,
See the contrast of what’s inside,
Because when it comes to how much I like you,
I have absolutely nothing to hide.

Yours, more than you know.

P.S. I think you already knew.
